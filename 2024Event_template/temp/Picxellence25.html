Pixcellence

What it’s all about.

Theme- “Innovanza Through Your Lens.”

Any technical event feels incomplete without some splendid clicks to cherish for a lifetime. Picxellence is such an event where you get to unleash your shutter skills and compete among the budding photographers of the institute. This event shall be included as a competitive and fun event.

Event Guidelines

Participants are introduced to Innovanza and the theme.
Participants have to register for the event.
Participants are required to click pictures during the events of Innovanza’25.
Then they need to submit their entries via the Google form provided.
The jury will evaluate and finalize the winning entries.

Rules and Regulations

1.Participants must adhere to the event timeline and submission guidelines.

2.All entries must be original works created during Innovanza'25.

Reference Image

Judging Criteria

1. The submitted entries shall be judged on the basis of creativity Picture composition Photography skills relevance to theme and overall appeal.
