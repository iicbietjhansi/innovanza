Junk Yard

What it’s all about.

Get ready to unleash your creativity! Junk Yard is an offline event about turning scraps into something amazing with your hands. In this event, a video of a cool and fun project will be demonstrated to participants, and they must recreate it using the materials they have been provided within the given time.
Whether you match the design perfectly or add your unique twist, this event is about having fun, being creative, and bringing ideas to life!
roadmap

Flow Of Event

Registration & Team Formation

• All Participant Must Register for the Event.
Project Making

•All teams will receive the necessary materials and tools for the project through an exciting game.
•Participants will be given 1 hr to recreate the project.
Submission

• All Participant must submit there Project before deadline.
• After that participants will present the project and be judged based on judging criteria.

Rules and Regulations

• Participants must recreate the project as shown in the video.

• Only materials provided by the organizers are allowed to be used.

• Projects must be completed within the allotted time.

• Mobiles will not be allowed during the event.

• Any form of misconduct or violation of rules will lead to disqualification.

Judging Criteria

1. Accuracy:
• How closely the project matches the design shown in the video.
2. Creativity:
• Innovative ideas, and unique touches added to the project.
3. Functionality:
• How well the project performs.
4. Presentation:
• Overall finish and decoration of the project.
5. Time Management:
• Completion of the project within the given timeframe.
