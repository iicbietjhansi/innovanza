MEME-E-THON

What it’s all about.

Get ready to showcase your creativity and humor at this year's college fest with Meme-e-thon! Whether you're capturing funny moments from events or adding a humorous twist to the fest's overall vibe, it's all about making people laugh! In this online event, participants will create original memes from photos or videos. The memes will be judged on creativity, humor, and relevance to the theme. Let your memes go viral. Join us and see who can craft the funniest meme of the fest!

Rewards

A panel of judges reviews submissions based on creativity, humor, originality, and relevance to the event.

Rules and Regulations

• Memes must be original and created by the participants.

• Only content related to Innovanza’s events, competitions, or highlights can be used.

• Memes must adhere to content guidelines (no vulgar, offensive, or inappropriate material).

• Each submission must follow the specified format.

• Memes must be submitted by the given deadline.

• Any violation of the rules may result in disqualification.

Judging Criteria

1. Creativity and Originality:
How original and unique the meme is.
2. Relevance to Theme:
How well the meme relates to the theme and Innovanza events.
3. Overall Impact:
Judges will consider the overall impact and effectiveness of the memes in entertaining and engaging the audience.
4. Judges' Discretion:
Judges may also consider additional factors such as humour, wit, and visual appeal in their assessment
