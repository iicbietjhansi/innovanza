Flow of Event

Registration
Participants first need to register for the event and they will be informed about the events and rules.
Team Making
Each match will consist of 25 teams competing in a given map.
Prize Distribution
Top 3 among the final 4 teams left will be declared as winner and runner ups respectively.

Rules and Regulations

1. Students from 9th to 12th standard or any recognized institution can participate.

2. No external equipment allowed.

3. Players must maintain respectful conversation; no abuse or vulgarity.

4. Camping is prohibited.

5. Jury's decision is final.

6. Top 3 teams will be declared winner, runner-up, and second runner-up.

Point System

1. Each match will consist of 25 teams competing in a given map. The official point system will be followed.

2. Kill Points: 1 point per kill.    3. 1st Position: 15 points

4. 2nd Position: 12 points.    5. 3rd Position: 10 points.

6. 4th Position: 8 points.    7. 5th Position: 6 points.

8. 6th Position: 4 points.    9. 7th Position: 2 points.

10. 8-12th Position: 1 point.    11. 13-25th Position: 0 points.

Judging Criteria

1.Participants first need to register for the event and they will be informed about the events and rules.

2.The team with the highest points at the end will be declared the winner, followed by the runner-up based on their points.
